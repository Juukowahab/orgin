Boundary Survey Services

What is a Boundary Survey?

A boundary survey determines and documents the exact location of property lines and corners. This essential service helps property owners understand their land boundaries and avoid potential disputes.

When Do You Need a Boundary Survey?

  • Before buying or selling property
  • When planning construction near property lines
  • To resolve boundary disputes
  • Before installing fences or landscaping

Our Process

  1. Research existing property records and deeds
  2. Field survey using state-of-the-art equipment
  3. Analysis of collected data
  4. Setting or verifying property corners
  5. Preparing detailed survey drawings
